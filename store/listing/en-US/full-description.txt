PrismTask is a powerful, beautifully designed task manager and habit tracker that adapts to how you work.

SMART TASK MANAGEMENT
Create tasks with natural language — just type "Buy groceries tomorrow !high #errands" and PrismTask parses it instantly. Organize with projects, tags, subtasks, and priorities. Drag to reorder, bulk edit, and quick-reschedule with a long-press.

TASK TEMPLATES
Save reusable blueprints for recurring workflows. Start with 6 built-in templates (Morning Routine, Weekly Review, Meeting Prep, and more) or create your own. One tap to create a fully structured task with subtasks.

RECURRING TASKS & REMINDERS
Daily, weekly, monthly, yearly — with custom intervals, day selection, and end conditions. Never miss a deadline with smart reminders delivered right on time.

HABIT TRACKING
Build lasting habits with streak tracking, analytics, and a GitHub-style contribution grid. See your 7/30/90-day completion rates and identify your most productive days.

MULTIPLE VIEWS
Today focus screen, task list, week view, month calendar, daily timeline, and Eisenhower Matrix. See your work the way that suits you best.

SMART FOCUS (PRO)
AI plans your Pomodoro sessions based on deadlines, priorities, and your preferred work style. Choose Deep Work, Quick Wins, Balanced, or Deadline-Driven focus modes.

AI EISENHOWER MATRIX (PRO)
AI automatically categorizes your tasks into Urgent/Important quadrants. Spend time on what matters, not on deciding what matters.

CLOUD SYNC (PRO)
Sync tasks across devices. Never lose your data with automatic cloud backup.

AI BRIEFING & WEEKLY PLANNER (PRO)
Get AI-powered daily briefings, plus a premium AI weekly planner backed by Claude Sonnet. AI time blocking automatically schedules your day for optimal productivity.

COLLABORATION (PRO)
Share projects with teammates or family. Assign tasks, leave comments, and track activity — all in real-time.

GOOGLE CALENDAR SYNC
Two-way sync between PrismTask and Google Calendar. See your calendar events alongside your tasks in every view.

HOME SCREEN WIDGETS
Today overview, habit streaks, and quick-add widgets for your home screen.

BEAUTIFUL & CUSTOMIZABLE
Material 3 design with dynamic colors, 12 accent color choices, and light/dark themes.
