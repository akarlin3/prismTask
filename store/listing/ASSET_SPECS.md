# Play Store Asset Specifications

## Required Assets

### App Icon
- **Size:** 512x512 PNG
- **Format:** 32-bit PNG, no alpha
- **Notes:** Already have custom icon — export at this size from the adaptive icon source

### Feature Graphic
- **Size:** 1024x500 PNG
- **Purpose:** Displayed at top of Play Store listing
- **Content:** App name + key screens + tagline
- **Design notes:** Should show the PrismTask brand, a device mockup with the app, and the tagline "Smart tasks, habits & AI focus planning"

### Screenshots
- **Minimum:** 4 screenshots
- **Recommended:** 8 screenshots
- **Phone format:** 16:9 aspect ratio, min 320px, max 3840px on short side
- **Recommended resolution:** 1080x1920 or 1440x2560

#### Suggested Screenshots
1. **Today Screen** — Progress ring with tasks and habits overview
2. **Task Editor** — Bottom sheet with Details/Schedule/Organize tabs
3. **Eisenhower Matrix** — 2x2 grid with AI categorization
4. **Smart Pomodoro** — AI focus session planning with task allocation
5. **Habit Analytics** — Contribution grid, streak stats, completion rates
6. **Week View** — 7-day column view with calendar events
7. **Template List** — Template library with categories and quick-use
8. **Quick-Add NLP** — Natural language parsing preview in action

### Notes
- Screenshots must be captured from the actual app on a real device or emulator
- Screenshots cannot be generated programmatically
- Consider adding a thin frame/mockup around screenshots for professional appearance
- Add captions/labels overlaid on screenshots highlighting key features
