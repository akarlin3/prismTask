## GitHub Release notes (longer-form)

# PrismTask v2.0.0

This release brings three themes: **smarter medication workflows**,
**privacy-first AI features**, and **time-boundary precision** so the app
respects your custom day-start hour everywhere it matters.

## What's new

**Bulk medication marking.** Mark every medication in a slot — or every
slot across today — to a single tier (skipped, essential, prescription,
complete) in one action. Both Android and web. The whole batch undoes
atomically via the existing 24-hour batch history.

**AI batch operations with natural language.** Type "I took my morning
meds" or "tag all Friday tasks as #personal" and PrismTask parses your
intent, shows you a preview of every change before applying, and lets
you undo with a 30-second snackbar. Medication mutations now join task
mutations in this flow, with a tag-diff preview showing exactly what
changes.

**Versioned built-in habit templates.** The six built-in habits (School,
Leisure, Morning/Bedtime Self-Care, Medication, Housework) auto-update
when we improve them. You see a diff, approve each change, and can
detach individual habits if you prefer your customized version.

**In-app account deletion (Android + web).** Delete your account from
Settings → Account & Sync. Takes effect immediately — sign-out + local
data wipe + a 30-day grace window where you can sign back in to restore.
After 30 days, your record is permanently purged from our database and
from Firebase Auth.

**Medication time logging.** Track when you actually took a dose vs.
when you logged it. The medication card now shows "Taken at 8:05 AM ·
Logged at 10:30 AM" when you backdate, plus a small clock icon on the
tier chip when there's a meaningful gap.

## What's better

**Start-of-Day boundaries everywhere.** If you've set a custom
Start-of-Day (e.g., 4 AM), the Today screen, task list grouping,
medication tracking, and Daily Essentials section now all reset at your
chosen time — not at calendar midnight. Open the app at 11 PM, leave it
open across 4 AM, and your task buckets and medication log roll over
correctly.

**Privacy controls for AI.** Settings → AI Features now has a master
"Use Claude AI for advanced features" toggle. When off, no PrismTask
data — including medication names — reaches our backend or Anthropic.
We've also updated our privacy policy to clearly disclose that
medication names travel in NLP batch commands.

**Cross-device medication sync.** Medications, slots, dose history, and
tier states sync bidirectionally now via both Firestore and the
backend, with append-only audit logging.

## Behind the scenes

**A unified day-boundary system.** We replaced ad-hoc "what day is it"
calculations across the app with a shared reactive helper that ticks at
every Start-of-Day boundary. Eliminates a whole class of "yesterday's
content shown as today's" bugs.

**Stronger privacy invariants.** New unit tests verify that the AI gate
short-circuits HTTP requests to Anthropic-touching endpoints when the
toggle is off — no relying on the backend alone to enforce the
contract.

**Medication tier alignment across platforms.** Web's medication tier
values now match Android's canonical model, enabling lossless
cross-device sync.

**Schema cleanup.** Dropped the orphan `medication_marks` table that
was never used in production. Cleaned up a sync-protocol footgun before
it could become a real bug.
