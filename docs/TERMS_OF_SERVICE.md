## 4. Free Tier

The free tier of PrismTask includes:

- Full task management with projects, tags, subtasks, and recurrence
- Reminders and notifications
- Habit tracking with streaks and analytics
- Task templates (6 built-in templates and custom templates, stored locally)
- Google Calendar sync
- Home screen widgets (Today overview, habit streaks, quick-add)
- Natural language quick-add (local parsing)
- All views: Today, Tasks, Week, Month, Timeline, and Eisenhower Matrix
- Drag-to-reorder, bulk edit, quick reschedule, and duplicate task
- JSON and CSV data export/import (local)
- Dark and light themes with accent color customization
- Sort preferences
